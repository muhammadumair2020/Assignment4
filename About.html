About Us

Mr Mohammad Umair as licensed Clearing Forwarding Agent established LMO in the year 2012. Prior to its establishment he was associated with this profession since 2007.He has 06-years experience to his credit.

Our experience in handling clearence of any merchandis, machinery, chemicals, materials and plastics has always been appreciated by our clients. We are fully conversant with Custom Laws, Rules and Regulations,and Classification in Custom Tariff, Nomenclatures and Import and Export Control Regulations etc.

We deal with all the matters relating to clearence of consigments or whatever our clients entrust. We prefer cliams whatever necessary and follow them,as well as pay our best attention on draft representation, appeals, and reision petition for our valued customers.

In order to provide effective and efficient services to our clients / associates, we offer to provide you one window servide i.e. from your shipment to the custom clearence by following and full filing all the legal requirements.
